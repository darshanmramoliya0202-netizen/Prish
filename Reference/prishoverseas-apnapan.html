A word from the founder

The reason we show you everything

Prish Patel — PrishOverseas, Surat
APEDA Reg. No. XXXXXXXXXX · IEC: XXXXXXXXXX
March 2026

When I started PrishOverseas in 2016, I was a small trader from Surat who had grown up watching my father buy cumin from the Unjha mandi. I knew the farmers. I knew how a good lot smells versus a bad one. But I didn't know how to prove that to a buyer in Dubai or London who'd never met me.

I learned the hard way. The first buyer I lost — a German food company — didn't say our product was bad. They said they couldn't verify us. No face behind the business. No farm photos. Just a PDF catalogue that looked like every other Indian exporter.

That changed everything. We started showing the imperfect truth: the Unjha mandi on a hot April morning, the sortex machine in our shed, a batch we rejected because moisture was 0.3% too high. We started putting our phone number everywhere. We started answering WhatsApp at 10pm.

Now this website is built on that same principle: you should be able to audit us before you buy from us. Our IEC number is real. Our lab reports are downloadable. Our farmers have names and faces. And if anything about our product ever disappoints you — you have my personal WhatsApp number on this page.

That is the only promise I know how to make.

Things we will tell you honestly
ON PRICING
Why don't you show prices on the website?
Agri commodity prices change daily with market conditions. A price we show today could be wrong tomorrow. We give you live pricing on WhatsApp — it takes 10 minutes and it's accurate.
ON MINIMUMS
What if my order is less than the MOQ?
Our MOQs exist because of container economics and lab testing cost per lot. For smaller buyers, we can sometimes accommodate smaller quantities in a mixed container. Ask us — we'll try to find a way.
ON REJECTIONS
What happens if a lot doesn't pass quality?
We reject it. This has happened 4 times in 2024–25. It delays delivery by 10–15 days. We tell the buyer immediately and explain why. We believe one honest delay is better than one disappointed relationship.
ON LIMITS
What products can't you supply?
We are focused on Gujarat-origin spices and Indian pulses. We do not handle fresh produce, processed foods, or commodities outside our expertise. Better to be honest than to say yes to everything.
